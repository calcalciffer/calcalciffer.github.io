Welcome to the Civilization Plus League

The Civilization Plus League (CPL) is a competitive league for Civilization VI players. Join us to compete, improve your skills, and enjoy the game with a community of like-minded players.

Getting Started

To get started, check out the 'About' section to learn more about the league, read the 'CPL Rules' to understand how the league operates, and explore the 'Seasons' to see current and upcoming competitions.

Stay Connected

Follow us on social media and join our Discord server to stay updated on league news, events, and discussions.
